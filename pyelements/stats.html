Complete Stats for Every Hero in Valve's Dota 2 Underlords

Chase Wassenar - June 13, 2019
Dota 2 Underlords is officially out for players who own the TI9 battle pass, bringing 60 heroes to Valve's new auto chess title. Unikrn has put together this companion piece to our Beginner's Guide to keep every relevant stat for all of the game's heroes in one spot for players looking to get the edge on the ranked ladder.

Valve has released the beta for their new Auto Chess title Dota 2 Underlords, giving players who purchased The International 2019's Battle Pass the chance to test out the game before its wider release.

With 60 heroes currently available in game, it can be hard to sort through each one and keep track of their individual stats. To that end, Unikrn has written up this companion piece to our Dota 2 Underlords Beginner's Guide for players who want to have the stats for every piece in one convenient place.
